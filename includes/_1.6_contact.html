Frågor & Svar

How many skips do I get?

You can skip a song up to six times per hour. This includes Shuffle Play for playlists and artists, as well the Radio feature.

Why am I hearing different tracks?

When you press play you’ll hear a mix of that album or artist plus similar tracks. You'll also receive some suggested tracks when listening to albums with less than 12 tracks.

What’s the difference between Free and Premium?

Offline mode, Spotify Connect, and high-quality streaming without ads are features only available with a Premium subscription. With a free account, you can still listen to playlists and full albums.
